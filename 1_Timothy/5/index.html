Let a widow be enrolled if she is not less than sixty years of age, having been the wife of one husband,

enrolled

This is the only New Testament use of the Greek word translated "enroll" (καταλέγω.)

Liddell & Scott

καταλέγω

  1. recount, tell at length and in order
  2. reckon up, tell in full tale
      reckon, count as
      conclude by enumeration
  3. enumerate, draw up a list, hence, enroll, enlist
  4. select

This word elicits the work of supporting someone in need. It takes reckoning, detailed attention, a full accounting.

Contrast Acts 6:1 παραθεωρέω.

Acts 6:1
Now in these days when the disciples were increasing in number, a complaint by the Hellenists arose against the Hebrews because their widows were being neglected in the daily distribution.
