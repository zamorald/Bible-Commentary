These they set before the apostles

While the entire congregation's input was solicited and valued, the final installment must be under apostolic authority.

laid their hands on them

Laying on of hands is an "elementary doctrine" according to Hebrews 6:1-3. It denotes approval, direct involvement, and selection or appointment.
