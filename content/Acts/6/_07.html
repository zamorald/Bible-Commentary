7
And the word of God continued to increase, and the number of the disciples multiplied greatly in Jerusalem, and a great many of the priests became obedient to the faith.

the word of God continued to increase

The powerful effect of following God's pattern cannot be overstated. The connection between feeding the widows and watching the word of God increase may not be obvious on first glance. Ancient Israel's military strategy was neither--they trusted in the Lord to provide the victory. So we today adhere to His word and see the results God can get and not man.
